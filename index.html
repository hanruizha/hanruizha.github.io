Hello! I am a visiting faculty researcher at Google Research NYC. Prior to that, I:

  • was a postdoctoral fellow at the Simons Laufer Mathematical Sciences Institute (SLMath, formerly MSRI), affiliated with UC Berkeley, in Fall 2023;

  • got my PhD in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University in 2023, advised by Vincent Conitzer;

  • interned multiple times at Google Research, mostly working with members and friends of the OMEGA Market Algorithms group led by Vahab Mirrokni;

  • did my undergraduate in Yao Class, Tsinghua University, where I worked closely with Wei Chen and Pingzhong Tang.

I am interested in Economics and Computation – problems with economic motivations that can be approached using techniques from computer science. My recent research focuses on learning and decision making in complex environments, in the presence of strategic behavior, with limited means of interaction, under uncertainty of the future.
